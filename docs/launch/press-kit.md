## 100-word description

ChessTrophies is a free, browser-based chess and checkers platform built by a solo developer. It runs in any modern browser — no download, no signup to start, installable as an app. Play ranked online 1v1, 2v2 team chess, and official checkers (8×8 ACF + 10×10), each with its own ELO, plus casual games and practice against a computer calibrated to your rating. Its signature loop: a 7-win streak mints a trophy listing the opponents you beat, and they see you on their "Most Feared" wall with a revenge rematch. Live Arena tournaments, a monthly Seasons ladder, adaptive puzzles, and 60+ trophies round it out.

## Feature list

- **Free to play, in-browser** — no download, no signup required to start; installable as a PWA.
- **Chess + checkers, both ranked** — online 1v1, 2v2 team chess, and official 8×8 (ACF) / 10×10 (international) checkers, each with a separate ELO.
- **Casual + practice modes** — unrated online games, and practice against a computer calibrated to your exact rating.
- **Always-on matchmaking** — when no human is queued, ranked backfills an honestly-labeled bot tuned to your rating (ELO-neutral, not farmable), so you always get a game.
- **Streak trophies + "Most Feared" wall** — a 7-win ranked streak mints a trophy naming your defeated opponents; they get a revenge rematch (in-app + Web Push).
- **Live Arena tournaments** — 3+2 blitz, continuous pairing, a live leaderboard with streak markers, and a champion title.
- **Monthly Seasons ladder** — a fresh competitive ladder each month, separate from your live ELO.
- **Adaptive puzzles + Puzzle Rush** — a personal puzzle rating, puzzles served near your level, and a 3-minute survival mode.
- **Daily-streak loop + reminders** — solving the daily puzzle keeps your streak alive; optional Web Push nudges.
- **60+ trophies & achievements** — wins, streaks, fast wins, comebacks, plus hidden trophies.
- **Post-game review** — eval bar, eval graph, and blunder/best-move analysis.
- **Themed piece & board sets** — board palettes and piece sets; premium themed sets available via an optional subscription.
- **Shareable challenge links** — beat the computer, then send a link that drops a friend into the same fight as a guest, with live "N tried, M beat it" social proof.

---

## Pricing

Free. An optional **Premium subscription ($4.99/month)** unlocks cosmetic themed piece/board sets and removes ads. No chess, checkers, ranked, tournament, or puzzle features are paywalled.
