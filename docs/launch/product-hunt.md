## Description (the listing body)

ChessTrophies is a free chess **and** checkers platform that runs entirely in your browser — no download, no account required to start, installable as an app if you want it.

Play ranked online 1v1 or 2v2 team chess with a real ELO, unrated casual games, or practice against a computer that's tuned to your exact rating. Because there's always someone (or a calibrated bot) to play, you can climb without waiting in an empty queue.

The hook is the trophy system: win 7 ranked games in a row and you mint a trophy engraved with the names of everyone you beat to get there. The players you defeat see you on their **Most Feared** wall and can fire back a revenge rematch. Add live Arena tournaments, a monthly Seasons ladder, adaptive puzzles with Puzzle Rush, a daily-streak loop, and 60+ earnable achievements, and casual games stop feeling disposable.

Built and maintained by one person. Honest about what it is: pre-launch, rough edges welcome.

## Feature highlights (5–7 bullets)

- 🏆 **Streak trophies that name your victims** — a 7-win ranked streak mints a trophy listing every opponent you beat; lose and you land on their **Most Feared** wall with a revenge rematch waiting.
- ♟️ **Chess + checkers, both ranked** — online 1v1, 2v2 team chess, and official 8×8 (ACF) / 10×10 checkers, each with its own ELO.
- 🤖 **Always a game** — ranked bot-backfill matches you with a computer calibrated to your rating when no human is queued, so the ladder never stalls (and it's ELO-fair, not farmable).
- ⏱️ **Live Arena tournaments + monthly Seasons** — drop into a 3+2 blitz arena with continuous pairing and a live leaderboard, win a champion title, and climb a fresh monthly ladder.
- 🧩 **Adaptive puzzles + Puzzle Rush** — a personal puzzle rating, puzzles served near your level, and a 3-minute survival mode; solving the daily puzzle keeps your streak alive.
- 🔗 **Challenge-a-friend links** — beat the computer, then send a link that drops a friend straight into the same fight as a guest (no signup), with live "N tried, M beat it" social proof.
- 🌐 **No download, no friction** — runs in any modern browser, installable as a PWA, free to play (optional Premium for themed sets + ad-free).
